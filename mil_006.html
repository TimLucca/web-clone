Ski-Zette

*These documents require Adobe Acrobat Reader. If you do not have the reader, click here to download it.

The Ski-Zette was the Camp Hale publication that highlighted camp activities. The links below show the front pages of the newspaper (except where otherwise noted).

  • What's My Name - December 18, 1942
    This was the first camp publication before they named it Ski-Zette. Page 4 identifies a movie schedule for that month.
    Key Headlines: Camp Hale's New Service Club Will Have Gala Opening Monday Night, Title Wanted for Camp Paper
    5 pages.
  • Camp Hale Ski-Zette - January 15, 1943
    'Army Hour' Broadcast to Originate From Camp Hale Sunday, Will Feature Training of SKi Troops, also General Rolfe, Mountain Troops Go on Review for Visiting Newsreel Men
    3 pages.

    Hale Men Find Gen. Rolfe Ideal Mountain Leader, Parachutes for Pacifists, All-Camp Mardi Gras to be held May 1, 2
